Here's a quick rundown of my education chronolgy from I am at 5 years old :

KINDERGARTEN


Tadika An-Najaah

Tadika An-Najaah is my first school at 5 years old . My parents sent me to this kindergarten when I was 5 years old to expose me to the knowledge and learning of Islam at an early age. Still fresh in my mind, the moment when we first entered the school, we were placed in a room or like leaisure room and locked the gate in front of our parents, to train ourselves to be independent. Many students who initially cried when separated from their parents, after 10 minutes had passed, all calmed down and the situation became cheerful with the behavior of the children of teachers and parents.

KINDERGARTEN


Tadika KEMAS SK Lahar Budi

This kindergarten is my second school after An-Najaah Kindergarten. I was admitted to this kindergarten at the age of 6 years. PRIMARY SCHOOL


Sekolah Kebangsaan Alor Mengkudu

I studied at Sekolah Kebangsaan Alor Mengkudu starting at the age of 7 to 12 years old. This school is a primary school very close to where I live. This school is also a legendary school, this is because, my grandfather is also a former student of the school, as well as my father and my other uncles. Until today, my family still continues the legacy there. My youngest sister also went to school there. There are various sweet and bitter memories created in this school during the early childhood.

SECONDARY SCHOOL


Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Agama Yan

After graduating from SK Alor Mengkudu, I received an offer to study from Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Agama Yan. I studied at Yan Religious National High School from the age of 13 to 17 years old. This school is a high school far from where I live. This school is my last school that I love the most. There are various sweet and bitter memories that have been created in this school during the early adolescence. I still remember all the memories and experiences I have had throughout my studies at this school. The services of friends who helped me a lot in the past, I will never forget. Apart from that, this is also where I learned the various meanings of life, the meaning of friendship, the meaning of sincerity, the meaning of love with the Almighty and His Beloved, Prophet Muhammad SAW. Not to be forgotten is the line of teachers who always work hard to help and guide students to success.
